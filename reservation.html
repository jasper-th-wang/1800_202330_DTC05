Rule and regulations for RainMate

Respect Reserved Times: Please arrive on time for your reservation. Your umbrella rental is reserved for a specific time, and we may not be able to extend it if you arrive late.

Umbrella Return: Please return the umbrellas promptly at the end of your reservation time. Late returns may result in additional charges.

Damages: Any damage to the umbrellas during your rental period will be the responsibility of the renter. Please handle the umbrellas with care.

Weather Conditions: RainMate are subject to weather conditions. In case of inclement weather, the rental may be rescheduled or canceled, and you will be notified accordingly.

Cancellation: If you need to cancel your reservation, please do so at least 2 hours in advance. Late cancellations may result in a cancellation fee.

No Smoking: Smoking is not allowed near the rental umbrellas to prevent damage and maintain a safe and clean environment.

Emergencies: In case of emergencies or issues, please contact our staff for assistance.

Respect for Others: Please be considerate of other guests and maintain a peaceful and enjoyable atmosphere
