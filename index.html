How it works

Open our RainMate app, and voilà! We’ve connected you with a local vendors who are part of our umbrella-sharing network. With just a tap, you can rent an umbrella and pick it up at your convenience. Need to return it? No problem! Drop it off at any of our partner vendors. It's that easy!
